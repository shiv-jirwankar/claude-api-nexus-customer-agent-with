# Nexus Support Policies

## Response Time SLAs
- Priority High: First response within 1 hour
- Priority Medium: First response within 4 hours  
- Priority Low: First response within 24 hours

## Escalation Policy
Escalate to Tier 2 when:
- Issue persists after 2 resolution attempts
- Data loss is reported
- Security breach is suspected
- Customer is on Enterprise plan and issue affects production

## Refund Policy
- Refunds approved within 30 days of charge
- Partial refunds for downtime exceeding SLA by more than 2x
- No refunds for user-error related issues

## Communication Guidelines
- Always address customer by first name
- Acknowledge the inconvenience before providing solution
- End every response with a follow-up offer
- Never promise specific fix timelines without engineering confirmation
